## 🎯 The Vision
We spend hours staring at screens, forgetting to blink, which leads to severe digital eye strain and fatigue. **BlinkBreak** turns a healthy habit into an engaging experience. By leveraging AR face-tracking technology, the app gamifies the act of blinking, giving your eyes the break they deserve while you play.

## ✨ Key Features
- **AR Blink Detection:** Uses ARKit to accurately track user blinks in real-time.
- **Gamified Health:** Progress through levels and earn rewards simply by keeping your eyes healthy.
- **Eye Strain Relief:** Scientifically backed intervals to ensure your eyes rest and rehydrate.
- **Intuitive UI/UX:** Built entirely with SwiftUI for a smooth, native iOS experience.
